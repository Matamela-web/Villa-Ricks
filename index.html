About Villa Ricks

Villa Ricks is a proudly local event venue located in Tshikuwi, Limpopo. We offer a beautiful and professional space for all types of events — from private celebrations to large community gatherings. If you want a place that brings people together with class and comfort, Villa Ricks is the place to be.

  • 🎈 Community Gatherings
  • 🌟 Special Appearances by Celebrities
